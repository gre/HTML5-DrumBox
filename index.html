Welcome to an experimental drumbox made with HTML5 using audio and canvas tags.
Let's mix

The concept

  • Each alphabetic key made a sound : a kick, a snare, a clap, a voice,...
  • A real time visualizer displays you a sound representation.
  • A drumbox helps you to repeat drums in a loop.
  • You can also configure BPM and cut number of this dumbox.
